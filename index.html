About me

HI, Im Nikoloz (niki) Getsadze, I have been building digital products for almost 20 years now and professional graphic designer since 2011. On this portfolio website you can view selection of my design works done for past few years.

They all speak well of what I firmly stand for: quality, uniqueness, timelessness, excellence and beauty. I have designed many websites, brands, collaterals, campaigns and digital applications for companies and brands alike. My design vision is based on a so called swiis style design and modernism.
